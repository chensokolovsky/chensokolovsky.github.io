Third Piano Lesson
After mastering our first two lessons, we now turn to some more complicated rhythmic patterns. We introduce the Time Signature and Eight Notes.
 
 
How To Use:
Set the tempo to 80 and practice the melodies. Read the instructions and learn how to count each note’s duration using the built in click.
Each note shape has a different duration and each bar contains a certain amount of ‘quarter notes’. Learn all this by reading the instructions and practicing the new melodies. We also added some more chords for you to explore.

When Are You Done?
After being able to play all five songs at a tempo of 200, try to play them by heart. Using the Sound settings, turn the notes off, and the Chords off. Play along with the click without looking at the display.
