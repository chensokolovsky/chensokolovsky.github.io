Mood Sampler



General:

Mood sampler is a sound playing gadget for story telling, jokes and fun activity. You can change sounds, labels, and record your own sounds using the microphone (up to 5 user sounds)



Main View:

1. Setting screen: where you can reset sounds, get to this help page, view credits and rate the app

2. Category Sample Button: Single tap plays/stops the sound. Double tap opens up a view to change the sound and the icon.

3. Sound category: There are 5 categories: Laughter & Crowd, Love, Cinematic, Animal and User sounds. On the first four categories you can set only sounds from that category (up to 5 sounds per category at a time). On the last line, you can record your own sounds using the microphone (up to 5 sounds)

4. User Sound Button: Single tap plays a sounds you recorded. Double tap opens up a view to record your own sound using the microphone. Microphone permission must be granted. Maximum sample length is 3 seconds.

Change Category Sound: (Opens when you tap and hold a category sample button)

5. Close the panel without changing anything

6. Single tap to change the icon (Emoji is recommended)

7. Close the panel and save the changes

8. Select a sound from the current category sounds list

Record User Sound: (Opens when you tap and hold a user sound button)

9. The sound number you are recording (up to 5 sounds)

10. Start recording / Stop

11. Progress bar for recording / playing

12. mic input meter (active while recording)

13. Play the recorded sound
