Mood Sampler:
Is a 15-Button Sampler which lets you trigger sounds by hitting a button. You can set your buttons to play any sound from the list which includes Romantic sounds, Laughter Sounds, Thriller Efx, Animals and different Alarms and noises.
 
 
Purpose:
To be honest - there isn’t one. You can use it to make people laugh , add sounds to a story or a joke you are telling, add effects to your living room theatre show..
Tell us if you have ideas for more sounds that you can use !!
