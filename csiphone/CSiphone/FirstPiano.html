First Piano Lesson
is your first piano lesson. Learn To play different songs by playing along with a lighted keyboard witch shows you which notes to play.
 
 
How To Use:
Sit in front of your keyboard or piano at home, and locate your right hand on the keys as shown in the screenshot to the left. Set the tempo to 60 and set the song to ‘Mary’. Play the orange note using your fingers, Your hand should stay at the same location and  you should hit the keys by lowering your finger that fits the current key.

When Are You Done?
After being able to play all three songs at a tempo of 200, try to play them by heart. Using the Sound settings, turn the notes off, and play along with the click without looking at the display.
